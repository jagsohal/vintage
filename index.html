The Nitty Gritty

So, you have booked your venue and started to think about your styling, you may have begun to buy things from Ebay and have a small mountain of goodies, but what now?

You might not have done any of those things and need me to guide you from the beginning, either way I am here for you. I believe that your wedding should be a reflection of your personality and we don’t have to stick to the ‘norm’. Trends come and go but good styling will always remain and I am here to ensure that your dreams can be turned into reality.

So, whether it’s a wedding on a grand scale, or an intimate gathering of loved ones, The Vintage House That Could will be with you all the way to hold your hand. I realise the importance of having the WOW factor at your special event and the feeling of confidence that every little detail is taken care of (so that you can drink Champagne!) Together we will go through everything in detail from our initial meeting where we discuss your ideas all the way through until the big day.
